We are SETH VII, a digital art collective pushing the boundaries of cyberpunk aesthetics and grunge visual culture. Our work explores the intersection of digital technology and visceral, raw artistic expression. Every project is a statement—bold, unapologetic, and designed to provoke.

Our Creative Process

Visual Direction

We start by establishing a strong visual direction. Our styleframes explore color palettes, composition, and the raw energy we want to capture. Every frame is carefully crafted to evoke emotion and establish the aesthetic foundation for the entire project.

Character Development

Character design is where personality meets aesthetics. We develop unique characters that embody the spirit of our projects. From concept to final design, each character is a vessel for storytelling and visual impact.

Digital Animation

Animation brings our visions to life. We blend traditional animation principles with cutting-edge digital techniques to create movement that feels both organic and otherworldly. Every frame is deliberate, every transition purposeful.

Production & Rendering

The final stage is where everything comes together. We meticulously render and composite our work, ensuring every pixel contributes to the overall vision. Color grading, effects, and final touches transform raw animation into polished art.
