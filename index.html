We are SETH VII, a digital art collective pushing the boundaries of cyberpunk aesthetics and grunge visual culture. Our work explores the intersection of digital technology and visceral, raw artistic expression. Every project is a statement—bold, unapologetic, and designed to provoke.

Our Creative Process

Visual Direction

We start by establishing a strong visual direction. Our styleframes explore color palettes, composition, and the raw energy we want to capture. Every frame is carefully crafted to evoke emotion and establish the aesthetic foundation for the entire project.

Character Design

Characters are the heart of our work. We design with intention, creating personalities that resonate with the project's core message. From concept to final design, every detail matters—proportions, expressions, and the subtle nuances that bring characters to life.

Animation & Motion

Motion brings everything together. We craft animations that feel organic and visceral, breathing life into static designs. Every movement is purposeful, every transition deliberate. The animation process is where the magic happens.

Production Setup

Behind every digital creation is meticulous planning and technical execution. Our production setup ensures quality at every stage—from pre-visualization to final rendering. We document the journey because the process is as important as the product.
